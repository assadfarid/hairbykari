FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Are you familiar with different hair textures?

I am familiar and have worked with many textures/types of hair. Before any style/cut is done I like to have consultation before the appointment, so I fully understand the clients needs and ensure the clients expectations are met.

How long will it take to style my hair on my wedding day?

I allot the bride an hour and bridesmaids 45 min. I will also provide a hair schedule for the day of which includes some buffer time for anyone with thicker hair and touch-ups.

Are reservations recommended?

Due to high and low seasons, reservations are recommended to hold the date you are interested in. A deposit is collected to hold your date which is deducted off your total balance.

Is there a minimum booking amount for wedding parties?

There is a minimum booking amount of 5 clients or more. If you cannot book 5 clients, you will still be charged for that amount. Any overage above and beyond can be applied to hair accessories or reception touch ups/wait time. Your final quote can be made 4 months prior to your wedding.

Can I make changes to my booking after I reserve my date?

You can always add on additional hair styling services, but you cannot remove any. If you are unsure about guests just add them on later if needed. The getting ready location can be updated as well. A requote will be written up and sent out as well.

Is there a travel fee?

The travel fee is calculated from the artists home base. Any location under 20 minutes 1 way are complimentary. Anything over minutes a travel fee applies.

Can I book a trial before reserving my wedding date?

Trials are recommended but I cannot hold your date unless you have provided your deposit.

Do you have a studio I can come to?

I travel to clients for event hairstyling such as Weddings, Sweet 16s, Engagements, Proms, Special events, etc. For any color or haircut appointments, the client can come to the salon I work at in Metuchen NJ.
